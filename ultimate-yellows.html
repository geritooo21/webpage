ULTIMATE YELLOWS SMOOTHIE

Add to favorites

A delicious yellow smoothie rich in antioxidants like beta-carotene for improved circulation, eye health, and digestive health.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups vanilla almond milk
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup frozen pineapple
  • ½ cup yellow squash
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon bee pollen
  • ¼ inch ginger root, peeled
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder, optional for a protein boost

Instructions

  1. In a high speed blender, blend all ingredients together until smooth. Add ice to the blender and blend or you can serve over ice in a glass. Enjoy immediately.
